Programming for Everyone

Learning has been a lifelong passion of mine. Whether it be as a teacher or as a student I am always striving to better both myself and my community. Programming and website design have recently become one of my main focuses; unfortunately, I believe that many people have trouble getting their foot in the door--especially with more complex projects like game development.

With this site, I'm hoping to document my own growth, projects, and struggles in order to provide an in-depth look at the development process behind both software and websites. In doing so, I hope to inspire more people to pick up coding in some form and take pride in the little successes--and failures--along the way!
